# Research Materials

This folder contains materials used during the experiments
documented in `narrative_alignment/`.

Only materials related to **publicly released portions of the narratives**
are included here.
Unpublished or private narrative content is intentionally excluded.

The **evaluation question patterns** used in the experiments are provided
without restriction.
You are free to reuse, modify, or adapt these question patterns
for your own testing and research purposes.

These materials are shared for transparency and reference only.
They are not curated, cleaned, or guaranteed to support reproducibility.

Use at your own discretion.
